### Toubleshooting
- [How to adjust AKAI MPK MIDI velocity on the keyboard itself || increase key sensitivity || any DAW](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4iU8q_2CTpE) - press [_Full level_ for 5 seconds](https://www.reddit.com/r/akaiMPC/comments/kuvtz5/mpk_mini_3_velocity/), then adjust parameters with KNOB
	- If you want to reset them to their default values:
		- v1: 24.0  /  v3: 04.0 
        - v2: 06.0  /  v4: 02.4 
        - Black bal: 0.7
