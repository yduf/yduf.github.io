### [libfreenect](https://github.com/OpenKinect/libfreenect?tab=readme-ov-file#libfreenect)

A userspace driver for the Microsoft Kinect. It runs on Linux, OSX, and Windows and supports
- RGB and Depth Images
- Motors
- Accelerometer
- LED
- Audio
