### [Set as default shell](https://fishshell.com/docs/current/faq.html#faq-default)
{% highlight bash %}
chsh -s /usr/bin/fish
{% endhighlight %}

Check that fish is allowed in [`/etc/shells`](https://www.linuxfromscratch.org/blfs/view/5.1/postlfs/etcshells.html)
