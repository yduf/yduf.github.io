> `watch -n 5 --difference ls -l` # [Watch](https://linux.die.net/man/1/watch) every 5 seconds - ([SO](https://stackoverflow.com/a/13593790/51386))

If you wish to have visual confirmation of changes, append `--differences` prior to the ls command.
