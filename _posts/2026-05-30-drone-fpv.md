**Analog FPV**  
Very low latency  
Fuzzy, lower-resolution image  
Static and breakup are common  

**DJI O3**  
Excellent HD video quality  
More expensive than analog  
Being superseded by O4  

**DJI O4**  
Lower latency improvements  
Highest cost
