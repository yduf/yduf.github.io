Compatibility with Goggles depends on which [video transmitter (VTX)](https://chatgpt.com/share/6a1c083a-2338-83eb-8270-5470e44cf5c8) is installed in your drone.

**O4** > **O3** > **Analog** for most pilots.

**Analog FPV**  
Very low latency  
Fuzzy, lower-resolution image  
Static and breakup are common  

**DJI O3**  
Excellent HD video quality  
More expensive than analog  
Being superseded by O4  

**DJI O4**  
Lower latency improvements  
Highest cost
