$n = \sqrt{n^2}$ if n > 0

Lead to 
$n = \sqrt{n(n+1) - n}$ (if n > 0)
and replacing n ad nauseum

$n = \sqrt{n(n+1) - \sqrt{n(n+1) - \sqrt{n(n+1) - \sqrt{n(n+1) - n}...}}}$ (if n > 0)
