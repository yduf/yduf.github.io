## Spec

- CPU - Pentium Gold 7505 (64 bits @ 3,5 GHz) 
- Graphique UHD 
- RAM 16 Go LPDDR4x

- Wi-Fi 6E Tri Band (802.11ax)

- 2x USB 3.2 Type A
