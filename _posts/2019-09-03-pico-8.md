> [PICO-8](https://www.lexaloffle.com/pico-8.php) is a fantasy console for making, sharing and playing tiny games and other computer programs. It feels like a regular console, but runs on Windows / Mac / Linux. When you turn it on, the machine greets you with a commandline, a suite of cartridge creation tools, and an online cartridge browser called SPLORE. - [r/pico8](https://www.reddit.com/r/pico8/) / [Showcase](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oBFD-CxJorQ) / [Awesome PICO-8](https://pico-8.github.io/awesome-PICO-8/) / [online](https://www.pico-8-edu.com/)
