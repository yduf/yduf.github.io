> Tips and Tricks for Laser Marking your 3D Prints - [Laser Marking Prints 101](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FB31EQXS4bA)

- **laser:** IR - FIBER : ~1064nm wavelength (tatoo removal)
  - CO2 is out
- **head:** galvo
- **power** 2W to 5W are enough - max 20W
- **plastic:** PLA / PETGS / ABS / ASA 
  - not all brand work
